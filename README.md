The mod centers around the **Dupe Chest**, a custom block entity designed for end-game progression:

* **Left your stash behind?** Forgot your 67 stacks of Emerald Blocks at home? Don't worry, just dupe 'em!

To prevent world-breaking imbalances, the Dupe Chest requires a massive 9-item shapeless recipe consisting of the rarest artifacts in the game:

* **Ingredients**: Dragon Egg, Dragon Head, Mace, Silence Armor Trim, Music Disc (Pigstep), Conduit, Creeper Head, Piglin Head, and an Enchanted Golden Apple.
* **Recipe Book**: As soon as you obtain a **Dragon Egg**, the recipe will automatically unlock in your green recipe book.
